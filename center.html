ESG
affiliation

Pesto Green aims to make a sustainable lifestyle easier to access and adopt in Hong Kong. We serve as a platform to connect the local green service providers/merchants with sustainability practitioners, ranging from sustainability advocates to early adopters - who are new to the world of sustainable lifestyle.

Our qualified merchant partners will provide exclusive discounts for our platform users, where in return we will drive traffic to their business and enhance their goodwill in their sustainability commitment. We aspire to cover every aspect of products and services that people need on a daily basis.

Cryptocurrency
Issuance

Pesto Green aims to take the lead in the decentralization of the ESG commitment in which people have access and can participate directly in ESG acts in order to improve their lives and the lives of others. The Pesto Green Blockchain would be Solana-based, establishing a self-sustained ecosystem that is less energy intensive than existing Proof-of-Work (POW) blockchains. DAO governance structure will be incorporated, powering a fully-balanced reward mechanism.
